How Blicket Works

Step 1: Create an Event

Organize your event by entering details such as the event name, location, and time. The Blicket platform will generate a secure, blockchain-verified ticket for each attendee.

Step 2: Issue Tickets

Send digital tickets directly to attendees. These tickets are verified on the blockchain, ensuring authenticity and protecting against counterfeiting.

Step 3: Secure Ticket Transfers

Attendees can securely transfer or sell their tickets through the platform, without fear of fraud. Blockchain ensures that tickets are unique and non-duplicable.

Step 4: Analytics & Insights

Access a range of analytics and insights to track ticket sales, attendee engagement, and event success. Make data-driven decisions for your future events.
